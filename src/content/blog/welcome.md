Welcome. This is the first post on **kaanesendemir.com**.

I built this site as a home base: projects I can share cleanly on LinkedIn, notes on architecture and AI adoption, teaching, and the occasional longer thought that doesn’t fit a feed.

Here’s what you’ll find around the place:

- **Projects** — shareable builds and experiments with a proper link and preview image
- **Blog** — short writing like this, when something is worth saying in full sentences
- **Vision** — a private-ish board on how I’d lead a team (not indexed; living draft)
- **Tools** — personal prompts I reuse for architecture and teaching

I’m an Application Architect and Adjunct Professor. Most of my public work lives on LinkedIn; this site is the durable URL behind that.

If you’re here from a share — thanks for clicking through. More posts will land as Markdown files in the repo; each one gets a clean path, breadcrumbs, and a LinkedIn-ready preview.

Glad you stopped by.
