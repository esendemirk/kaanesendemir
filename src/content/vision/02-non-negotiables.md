---
title: Non-negotiables
order: 2
summary: The lines that don’t move when pressure shows up.
---

**People are not the backlog.** Urgency is real. Cruelty isn’t a delivery strategy.

**Judgment stays human.** Especially with AI. Tools accelerate; people decide what ships, what’s safe, and what’s worth doing.

**Say the hard thing early.** Ambiguity eats more time than a clear “no” or a clear “not yet.”

**We don’t perform busyness.** Busy calendars are not a proxy for impact. Outcomes are.
