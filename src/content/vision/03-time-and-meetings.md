---
title: Time and meetings
order: 3
summary: Boundaries that protect deep work and real life.
---

**No lunch meetings** as a default. Eat. Walk. Reset. If something is truly urgent, we name why — we don’t colonize the one break people have.

**No meetings after hours** unless production is on fire. Real incidents get a response. Habitual evening standups do not.

**Meetings earn their slot.** Agenda or it doesn’t happen. Decision or it becomes an email. “Quick sync” is not a strategy.

**Deep work is protected.** Focus blocks aren’t optional decoration on a calendar — they’re how architecture and teaching quality stay high.
